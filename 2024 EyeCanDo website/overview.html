What We Do?

EyeCanDo is the next generation communication platform to enable communication using eye gaze and brain computer interfaces (BCI), with affordable off-the-shelf devices. People with motor disabilities will be able to use the system to maximize their capabilities for communication using their choice of comfortable modality. 

Eye Gaze Tracking

EyeCanDo transforms eye movements into speech for ALS patients using accessible devices. It's a leap in assistive tech, enabling expression and interaction for those with motor limitations.

Navigating Accessibility

EyeCanDo's seamless integration of facial recognition and eye tracking technology enables effortless communication, transforming gazes into words on a virtual keyboard.

Connected Mobility

EyeCanDo unlocks new possibilities for hands-free digital engagement, offering a gateway to communication for patients

Enhanced Communication

EyeCanDo empowers ALS patients to communicate through eye gaze. Enter text by dwelling on keys or using gesture typing, and select phrases for quick text-to-speech conversion.
